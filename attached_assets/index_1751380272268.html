How to Use the Crochet Pricing Calculator

Calculator Tab

  1. Materials: Add all materials used in your project with their costs.
  2. Labor: Enter your hourly rate and the time spent on the project.
  3. Business Expenses: Include any overhead costs like tools, marketing, etc.
  4. Additional Factors: Set your profit margin, shipping costs, and tax rate.
  5. Calculate: Click the Calculate button to see your pricing breakdown.
  6. Save Project: Save your calculations for future reference.

Notion Tab

  1. Create a Notion page for tracking your crochet projects.
  2. Get the embed link from Notion (Share → Copy link → Enable public access).
  3. Paste the link in the Notion Embed URL field and click Embed.

Pricing Formula

The calculator uses this formula:

Base Cost = Materials + Labor + Business Expenses

Wholesale Price = Base Cost + (Base Cost × Profit Margin) + Shipping + Tax

Retail Price = Wholesale Price × 2
